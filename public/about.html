A bit about me

Growing up, I was always drawn to technology. I was that kid who loved tinkering with computers, curious about how things worked beneath the surface. Solving technical problems became second nature to me, and before I knew it, I fell in love with it. One of the moments that sparked my deep interest in this world was watching The Social Network. Seeing how an idea could be turned into something that changes the world fascinated me—it made me want to understand the mechanics behind technology and how to build something impactful.

University was a game-changer. Studying Information Systems at Universitas Tarumanagara gave me the foundation I needed, but it was my hands-on experience that truly shaped me. My internship at PT Fokus Digital Nusantara was a defining moment—I worked on GPS tracking system development, handling system maintenance, software optimization, and troubleshooting to improve real-time tracking efficiency. Seeing my contributions directly impact business operations fueled my passion even more.

I was hungry for more

I expanded my skills in UI/UX design using Figma, built an e-commerce platform from scratch, and strengthened my ability to analyze, design, and implement scalable tech solutions. Beyond the technical side, I honed my leadership and teamwork skills, serving as Vice Coordinator of Public Relations at KMK Adhyatmaka and participating in community-driven tech initiatives.

Now, I see technology as more than just code—it’s about creating solutions that drive impact. Trends will evolve, but the goal remains the same: to innovate, solve problems, and make technology work seamlessly for people. That’s what keeps me motivated.
